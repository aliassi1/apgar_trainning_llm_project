📚 Return & Exchange Policy – The Miracles Bookshop
At The Miracles Bookshop, we value your satisfaction and want to ensure a smooth and transparent return process. Please read our return policy below:

✅ Eligibility for Returns
Items must be returned within 7 days of purchase or delivery.

Books must be in their original condition — unused, unmarked, and undamaged.

Proof of purchase (receipt or order confirmation) is required for all returns.

❌ Non-Returnable Items
Sale or clearance items

Personalized or custom-ordered books

E-books or digital content

Items damaged due to misuse or negligence

🔁 Exchanges
We offer exchanges for books that were received damaged, defective, or incorrect.

Please contact us within 48 hours of receiving the order with photos of the issue.

💸 Refunds
If eligible, refunds will be processed to your original payment method within 5–7 business days after the returned item is received and inspected.

Shipping costs are non-refundable, unless the return is due to our error.

🚚 Return Process
Contact our support team via Instagram DM or email at [your@email.com].

Provide your order number and reason for return.

Our team will guide you through the return process, including where to send the item.

📦 Return Shipping
Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item was damaged or incorrect.

We recommend using a trackable shipping method to avoid lost returns.

If you have any questions, don’t hesitate to reach out. We're here to help you enjoy every page!
